Submission Requirments

  • In order to have your paper published to the IEEE Xplore digital library, it is required that you present your paper. If you do not make your presentation for any reason, your paper will be excluded from Xplore.
  • Your full paper in PDF format must be submitted no later than April 11, 2021 23:59 (GMT -0700) at this web site. Your paper must comply with the specifications outlined on the web site. Due to publication deadlines, no time extensions will be granted.
  • As you prepare your final submission, make sure to carefully implement comments for improvements as suggested by reviewers. The Technical Program Committee cannot give any further clarification and you do not need to explicitly respond to them.
  • Your final paper will be submitted for plagiarism checking to the IEEE Crosscheck system.
  • Your completed IEEE copyright release must be filed by April 11, 2021 23:59 (GMT -0700). After logging into your author account, click the red "X" in the copyright column of your accepted paper & follow on-screen instructions. This will begin the digital filing process for your copyright release.

Xplore Compliance Procedure

It is required that all PDF submissions be IEEE Xplore compliant. If your file does not meet Xplore compliance it will not be published. To help you meet this requirement, you must FIRST check to ensure that your PDF file is compliant by using the IEEE PDF eXpress system BEFORE you submit your paper to the conference final submission web site.

  • Go to the IEEE PDF eXpress web site
  • Use this conference ID: TBD
  • Use PDF eXpress to check or create your PDF file
  • Once you have a GOOD PDF file, return to the AICAS 2021 final submission web site and upload your final submission.

NOTE: checking your PDF file for compliance and submitting your final paper for publication are two separate procedures. After using the IEEE PDF eXpress system for file compliance, MAKE SURE to return to the AICAS 2021 final submission web site and upload your compliant final submission.

Author Video Instructions

Registration for the meeting is required for you to present your work. In order to guarantee inclusion in the conference program one author must be registered by April 11, 2021 23:59 (GMT -0700).

As a basis for the live programming, please be available and be ready to present and answer a live Q&A with attendees following your talk. We request a prerecorded version of your presentation be submitted.

  • A 3-minute Introduction & Overview pitch will be played back via Zoom with a live introduction by a Session Chair and a live Q&A following the playback.
  • The full presentation will be posted "On Demand" for attendees to view at their leisure.

The videos will be posted within your presentation time slot on the AICAS 2021 on-demand virtual platform. Please note that the files must be a video file in MP4 format (more details below). Please do not try to upload PowerPoint or PDF files.

Our recommended approach to record a compatible video from the content displayed on your computer screen and voice recorded via the computer microphone, is to record the presentation using Zoom, available to users on most platforms and in most countries.
